## 1. Connecting WhatsApp

### What You Need
- A WhatsApp Business Account (get one free at [business.facebook.com/wa](https://business.facebook.com/wa))
- A phone number for your business (can be a new number)

### Step-by-Step

1. Go to **Settings** → **Integrations**
2. Click the **Connect WhatsApp** button
3. A new window will open asking you to login to Facebook
4. Login with the Facebook account that manages your WhatsApp Business Account
5. Review the permissions — we need:
   - Send and receive messages
   - Manage your WhatsApp Business account
6. Click **Continue** to authorize
7. You'll be redirected back to Signhify CRM

**You'll see a green "Connected" badge when it's working.**

### How It Works (Simple Explanation)

- When a customer sends a message to your WhatsApp number, it comes to Signhify CRM
- You reply from your computer — the message goes back through WhatsApp
- All conversations are saved in your inbox
- You can assign conversations to your team members

## 2. Connecting Instagram

### What You Need
- An **Instagram Business** or **Creator** account (not a personal profile)
- A **Facebook Page** that's linked to your Instagram account

### Convert Your Instagram to Business (if needed)

1. Open the Instagram app on your phone
2. Go to **Settings** → **Account** → **Switch to Professional Account**
3. Choose **Business** or **Creator**
4. Follow the steps to connect it to your Facebook Page

### Step-by-Step

1. Go to **Settings** → **Integrations**
2. Click **Connect Instagram**
3. Login with Facebook (must be the admin of the Facebook Page linked to your IG)
4. Authorize permissions:
   - Read and respond to messages
   - View your Instagram Business account
5. You're redirected back — **Connected** badge appears

### What Changes

- Instagram DMs from customers appear in your Signhify inbox
- You can reply from your computer
- Messages are marked with an Instagram icon so you know which channel they came from

## 3. Setting Up Email

### What You Need
- An email account (Gmail, Outlook, or any provider)

### Quick Setup (Gmail Example)

1. Go to **Settings** → **Email Settings**
2. Enter:
   - **Host**: `smtp.gmail.com`
   - **Port**: `587`
   - **User**: `your.email@gmail.com`
   - **Password**: Use an **App Password** (see below)
3. Click **Save & Test**

### How to Get a Gmail App Password

1. Go to [myaccount.google.com/security](https://myaccount.google.com/security)
2. Turn on **2-Step Verification** (if not already on)
3. Go to **App Passwords** (search for it in settings)
4. Select **Mail** and your device
5. Copy the 16-character password it shows you
6. Paste it into Signhify CRM's password field

> **Important**: Use the 16-character App Password, not your regular Gmail password.

### What You Can Do With Email

- Create email campaigns to your contacts
- Send automated follow-up emails
- Track who opened your emails
- Schedule emails to send later

## 6. Troubleshooting Common Issues

### WhatsApp Won't Connect

| What's Wrong | What to Do |
|-------------|-----------|
| You don't have a WhatsApp Business Account | Create one at [business.facebook.com/wa](https://business.facebook.com/wa) |
| You see "No accounts found" | Make sure your Facebook account manages a WhatsApp Business Account |
| "Access Denied" error | You need admin permissions on the WhatsApp Business Account |
| The page just spins | Close the popup, disable popup blocker, try again |

### Instagram Won't Connect

| What's Wrong | What to Do |
|-------------|-----------|
| "No Instagram accounts found" | Your Instagram must be a Business/Creator account, not Personal |
| "No Facebook Page linked" | In Instagram Settings, connect your account to a Facebook Page |
| "Access Denied" | You need to be admin of the Facebook Page that's linked to Instagram |
| Instagram DM replies fail | Make sure you've accepted Meta's Business Messaging terms |

### Email Not Working

| What's Wrong | What to Do |
|-------------|-----------|
| "Login failed" | For Gmail: use an App Password, not your regular password |
| Email not sending | Check your SMTP settings (host, port, user, pass) |
| Emails going to spam | Add a proper "From Name" and verify your email setup |
| Test email works but campaign doesn't | Check your recipient filter — it might be filtering everyone out |

### General

| Issue | Fix |
|-------|-----|
| Page not loading | Refresh the page |
| "Something went wrong" | Try again after 30 seconds |
| Can't see new messages | Click the refresh button in your inbox |
| Not getting notifications | Check browser notification permissions |
| Team member can't see conversations | Go to Settings > Team and add them to your workspace |
